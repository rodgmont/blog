## The beginning

There’s a question that followed me for years: *why build if you don’t understand the problem?*

I don’t start with theory. I start with a real, concrete problem—often personal—and then look for the right tool. Sometimes it’s code. Sometimes it’s a conversation. Sometimes it’s writing to organize ideas.

My name is Fran Rodgmont. I’m an engineer, a builder, and—according to the people who know me—someone who thinks out loud a bit too much.

---

## What do I do?

I build software. But more than that, I build **systems that solve things**.

- **Software engineering** → architecture, backend, performance
- **Product** → why does this exist? who uses it?
- **Open source** → I believe in building in public
